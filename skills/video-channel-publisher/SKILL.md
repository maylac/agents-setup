# Video Channel Publisher

## Workflow

1. **Read the asset**
   - Topic, audience, runtime, aspect ratio, language, CTA, and risk level.
2. **Choose channels**
   - Read `references/channel-matrix.md`.
   - Pick one primary channel and 1-3 secondary channels.
3. **Adapt the package**
   - Title options, description, captions, hashtags, thumbnail brief, pinned comment, and cross-post copy.
4. **Check readiness**
   - Verify the file exists, runtime/aspect ratio fit, captions/subtitles, CTA, and sensitive information.
5. **Approval gate**
   - Do not publish, schedule, upload, or post externally unless the user explicitly approves the exact final package, channel, account, and timing.
   - If approval is missing, stop at a publish-ready package.

## Safety

- Treat account actions as high-impact. Ask before publishing.
- Preserve platform terms and user privacy.
- Avoid medical, legal, financial, or income claims unless sourced and caveated.
- For client-like examples, anonymize unless the user confirms public permission.
